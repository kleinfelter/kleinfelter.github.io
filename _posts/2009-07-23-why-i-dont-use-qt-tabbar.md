There is a Windows utility, QT Tabbar, that gives Windows Explorer a tabbed interface.
  <br />
  <br />It mostly works, but I was seeing more Explorer flakiness than usual when I was using QT Tabbar, so I decided to remove it.&nbsp; 
  <br />
  <br />&quot;Remove it.&quot;&nbsp; Such a simple concept.&nbsp; So easy in theory (run the uninstaller); so hard in practice (no uninstaller).
  <br />
  <br />I had to resort to searching for files in C:\Windows (and below) containing &quot;QT Tabbar&quot; and searching the registry for &quot;QT Tabbar&quot; to remove it.
  <br />
